This system allows you to efficiently track and manage issues within your projects.

Our features include ticket creation, search and update functionalities, priority setting, and developer assignments.

Learn More

Features

Developer Accounts

Manage individual accounts for each developer. Ensure developers have access to the right tools and resources. Track their contributions and monitor their activity logs to ensure accountability and transparency.

Ticket Management

Create, update, and search for tickets with ease. Our system allows detailed ticket descriptions, tagging, and categorization to streamline issue tracking and resolution.

Priority Setting

Set priorities for tickets to ensure critical issues are addressed first. Customize priority levels to match your workflow needs and ensure that the most important tasks are highlighted and resolved promptly.

Assign/Unassign Tickets

Assign tickets to developers or allow them to self-assign. This feature helps in balancing the workload among team members and ensures that issues are addressed by the right personnel.

File Uploads

Upload patches or other required files when resolving issues. This enables easy sharing of resources and documents needed to troubleshoot and fix issues, ensuring everyone has the latest information.

Activity Log

Maintain a history of ticket activities, including comments and updates. This comprehensive log helps track progress and decisions made, providing a clear audit trail for each issue.

Customizable Workflows

Define and customize workflows to match your team's processes. Ensure that tickets move through the correct stages from creation to resolution, with clearly defined steps and responsibilities.

Real-time Notifications

Receive real-time notifications for ticket updates, comments, and assignments. Stay informed about the status of your issues and ensure timely responses and resolutions.

Analytics and Reporting

Generate detailed reports and analytics to understand trends, performance, and bottlenecks. Use this data to make informed decisions and improve your issue management process.

Integration with Third-party Tools

Integrate with popular third-party tools such as Slack, GitHub, and JIRA. Enhance your workflow by connecting our issue tracking system with other tools you use daily.
